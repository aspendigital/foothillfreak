Huntsman Hill

Two small canyons south of Immigration Canyon, Huntsman Hill provides 1000 vert of suprisingly steep foothill skiing.

Huntsman Hill as seen from the east. The steep north east gulley, hidden from view in the valley, is visible from this perspective.
The loop is merely a suggestion that would obviously require previous consent to tresspass in Huntsman's private canyon.
Sign off before dropping in.
Take time to watch the sun set before you enter the twilight bushwacking hell at the bottom.
