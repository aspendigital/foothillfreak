Timpanogos
It's a long way up, but the trail from timpanooke is so low grade that you hardly notice that you're climbing: perfect for legs not used to carrying skis and boots on the backpack. Managed to ski down around 2000 vertical after a vertical climb of 4000. Probably the best time to hit this line is closer to memorial day. Then there would be enough snow to ski straight down the upper scout falls. Or, ski this line later when there's really nothing else left to ski..
