A Perfect day at Perfect North Slopes


I knew no one would believe me so I had to take a picture of the massive avalanche that occured at Perfect North Slopes, near Lawrenceburgh, Indiana. If you look closely, you will see that the 4th lift tower up on the left side has been knocked over. The second one on the right was also damaged (see the crew working on it) but you can't tell from this perspective. Skiing in Indiana is super gnarly.
